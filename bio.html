About Mitchell

Mitchell Heidegger is the owner and licensed electrical contractor of Heico Electric Inc., a trusted electrician serving Kalispell, Whitefish, Bigfork, Lakeside, Somers, Polson, and communities throughout Northwest Montana. Heico Electric specializes in residential and light commercial electrical services, delivering safe, code-compliant installations with a strong focus on quality workmanship and dependable service.

With experience in new construction wiring, home remodels, electrical panel upgrades, service upgrades, lighting installation, troubleshooting, and general electrical repairs, Mitchell works directly with homeowners, builders, and business owners to provide efficient, long-lasting electrical solutions. Every project is completed with attention to detail, clear communication, and a commitment to doing the job right the first time.

As a locally owned and operated electrical company in Northwest Montana, Heico Electric understands the needs of homes and businesses in the Flathead Valley. Whether you need a licensed electrician in Kalispell, a panel upgrade in Whitefish, or electrical work for a new build in Bigfork, Heico Electric delivers professional results you can trust.
