About Bradley

Bradley Babcock is the kind of electrician you want on your jobsite—steady, detail-oriented, and proactive. As part of Heico Electric Inc., he helps homeowners, builders, and small businesses across Northwest Montana get clean, safe, code-compliant electrical work done right.

Bradley thrives on the “make it right” parts of the trade: clean panel work, tidy rough-ins, thoughtful device layouts, and troubleshooting that actually gets to the root cause. Whether it’s a new build, a remodel, or a service call, he brings a calm, no-drama approach—and keeps the work area organized.

Customers and contractors appreciate Bradley’s communication and follow-through. He shows up prepared, explains options in plain English, and finishes with the same attention to detail he started with.
